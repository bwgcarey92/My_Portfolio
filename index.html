About the BLAKE

Hi there! I'm Blake, a 31-year-old from the beautiful state of Michigan. I embarked on a transformative journey after attending Michigan State University, where my passion for coding was ignited. As someone who is always eager for new challenges, I decided to pivot my career and pursue my interest in technology. Over the past few years, I've been on a dedicated learning path to master the art of coding and web development. This journey has not only opened up exciting possibilities for my professional life but has also been a source of immense personal growth. My time at MSU provided a strong foundation, but it's the world of coding that has truly captured my imagination. I've embraced the challenges and joys of learning languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and the thrill of bringing ideas to life through code is incredibly rewarding. Outside of coding, you'll find me exploring the scenic landscapes of Michigan, enjoying the diverse culture, and, of course, cheering on the Spartans! I believe in the power of continuous learning and am excited about the endless possibilities that the tech world offers. Join me on this journey of growth and innovation as I shape a new career and embrace a life filled with creativity and endless possibilities.
